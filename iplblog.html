KXIP vs RCB DREAM11 Team Prediction: Sept 23rd, 2020

Title description, Sep 2, 2017

KXIP has been unable to register a win over RCB for quite a while. Their last dismissal of RCB was on 5 May in the 2017 IPL where KXIP made 138/7 after losing the toss and going in to bat first. RCB was all out for 119 in 19 overs. RCB have dominated the Kings in the last few matches. Will they be able to do it again? They have to consider the returning talent on board the Punjab team – Glenn Maxwell. Back fresh from their recent victory over SRH where Saini and Chahal did a good job for RCB in yesterday’s match – Will they repeat it?
